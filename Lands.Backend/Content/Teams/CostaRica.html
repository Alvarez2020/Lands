Day 1: Fly to Costa Rica

Board your flight and fly to Costa Rica
Bienvenidos a Costa Rica! Meet your Costa Rican Prométour Tour Director upon arrival
Dinner and overnight accommodation at El Rancho Lodge

Day 2: Tortuguero

Breakfast at the hotel
Transfer to Tortuguero – Famous for being the Western Hemisphere’s most important nesting site for the endangered Green Sea Turtle
Lunch at a local restaurant
Guided tour of Tortuguero National Park
Visit of the Sea Turtle Conservancy Museum
Dinner and overnight accommodation in Tortuguero.

Day 3: Sarapiquí

Breakfast at the hotel
Enjoy a 3-hour cruise on the Tortuguero Canals – There is plenty of wildlife to be spotted
Lunch at a local restaurant
Transfer to Sarapiquí – Known for its amazing and rich biodiversity as well as for being an adventure seeker’s dream destination
Dinner and overnight accommodation in Sarapiquí

Day 4: Sarapiquí

Breakfast at the hotel
Enjoy a zip line tour – Fly through the forest from platform to platform built on 60 to 90-foot trees
Lunch at a local restaurant
White Water Rafting on the Sarapiquí River
Dinner and overnight accommodation in Sarapiquí

Day 5: La Guacima

Breakfast at the hotel
Guided tour of the Tirimbina Rainforest Center – Learn about the flora and fauna of Costa Rica
Lunch at a local restaurant
Enjoy a horseback riding tour through trail rides of pasture, jungle and river
Dinner and overnight accommodation at El Rancho Lodge

Day 6: La Guacima

Breakfast at the hotel
Visit of La Paz Waterfall and Gardens – Where you can observe many different species of local wildlife
Lunch at a local restaurant
Visit of Poás Volcano – Considered one of the largest active craters in the world
Dinner and overnight accommodation at El Rancho Lodge

Day 7: Fly back Home

Breakfast at the hotel
Transfer to the airport
Board your flight from San José and fly back home
